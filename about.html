About

The eye
behind the lens.

Nicholas Caro is a Wiltshire-based photographer specialising in country sports, equestrian, and sports-action photography. Based in the UK and shooting across the Home Counties, the Cotswolds, and the West Country — from hunter trials in Wiltshire to polo at Cirencester and point-to-points across Gloucestershire and Oxfordshire.

Photography has been a constant since childhood — a discipline that rewards patience, precise timing, and an eye trained to anticipate rather than simply react. That instinct, sharpened through deliberate practice, has become a craft for capturing decisive moments in conditions that rarely cooperate.

An international career in retail technology consulting — spanning roles from Business Analyst through to Vice President of Professional Services — has built habits that translate directly into this work. The ability to read a situation quickly, understand what a client actually needs rather than what they ask for, and deliver under pressure are not peripheral advantages in field photography. They are prerequisites.

The images on this site are available for personal and commercial licensing and as prints. Each is offered on the basis that it earns its place — technically sound, compositionally considered, and suited to its intended use.
