Voice & Tone

Warm but not informal. Write as you would speak to a client you respect — clear, confident, unhurried.

Specific over vague. "Images delivered within 48 hours" not "fast turnaround." "Hunter trial and point-to-point" not "equestrian events."

Subject-literate. Use correct field sports terminology — point-to-point, not horse race; hunter trial, not show jumping. This signals authenticity to your audience.

Active voice. "I photograph equestrian events" not "Equestrian events are photographed." You are the subject.
